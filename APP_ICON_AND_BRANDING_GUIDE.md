### Step 5: Add Text (Optional)

2. Check contrast (should be clear at small size)

## ğŸ’¡ Design Tips

### Do's âœ…
- **Keep it simple** - Icon should be recognizable at 60Ã—60px
- **Use brand colors** - Maroon and gold consistently
- **Test at small sizes** - View icon at 60px, 40px, 29px
- **High contrast** - Ensure readability
- **Professional quality** - Invest time in polish
- **Follow guidelines** - Apple and Google have specific requirements

### Don'ts âŒ
- **Don't use photos** - Icons should be graphic, not photographic
- **Don't add text** in icon (it won't be readable at small sizes)
- **Don't use gradients** excessively (can look muddy at small sizes)
- **Don't copy** other church app icons exactly
- **Don't use complex details** (simplify for clarity)
- **Don't ignore safe areas** (especially for splash screen)
