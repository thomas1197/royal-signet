**Stripe, Inc.**
Website: https://stripe.com
Privacy Policy: https://stripe.com/privacy
Terms: https://stripe.com/legal

### 4.2 Accepted Payment Methods
- Visa
- Mastercard
- American Express
- Discover
- Other cards supported by Stripe

**Note:** We do NOT accept:
- Cash through the App (cash donations can be made in person)
- Checks through the App (checks can be mailed or delivered in person)
- Cryptocurrency (at this time)

### 4.3 Payment Security
- **We do NOT store your credit card information** on our servers
- All payment data is transmitted directly to Stripe using industry-standard encryption (TLS 1.2+)
- Stripe handles all sensitive payment information in compliance with PCI-DSS standards

### 4.4 Transaction Fees
**Royal Signet Church pays all processing fees.** Your full donation amount goes to the church, and we cover the Stripe processing fees (2.9% + $0.30 per transaction).

**Example:** If you donate $100, the church receives $100, and we pay approximately $3.20 in fees to Stripe.

## 6. Refund Policy

### 6.1 General Policy
**All donations are final and non-refundable.** Donations are voluntary contributions to support the church's mission and are not payments for goods or services.

### 6.2 Exceptions
We will issue refunds ONLY in the following circumstances:

**Duplicate Charges:**
- If the same donation is charged multiple times due to technical error
- We will refund duplicate charges promptly

**Unauthorized Transactions:**
- If your payment method was used without your authorization
- You must report unauthorized transactions within 60 days
- We may require documentation or a police report for fraud claims

**Accidental Donations:**
- If you accidentally entered the wrong amount (e.g., $1000 instead of $100)
- You must request a refund within **7 days**
- We reserve the right to verify the circumstances

### 6.3 How to Request a Refund
To request a refund, contact us within the applicable time period:

**Email:** donations@royalsignet.church
**Subject Line:** "Donation Refund Request"
**Include:**
- Your name and email address
- Date and amount of donation
- Transaction ID (from receipt email)
- Reason for refund request
- Documentation (if applicable)

**Processing Time:** Refund requests are reviewed within 5-7 business days. Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5-10 business days.

## 7. Tax Deductibility

### 7.1 Tax-Exempt Status
Royal Signet Church is a 501(c)(3) tax-exempt organization. Donations are tax-deductible to the maximum extent allowed by law.

**Tax ID (EIN):** [Insert EIN Number]

### 7.2 IRS Requirements
According to IRS regulations:
- Donations of **$250 or more** require a written acknowledgment for tax deduction purposes
- Donors are responsible for determining the deductibility of contributions
- Consult a tax professional for specific tax advice

### 7.3 Donation Receipts
**Automatic Receipts:**
- You will receive an email receipt immediately after each successful donation
- Receipts include:
  - Donation date
  - Donation amount
  - Donation type
  - Transaction ID
  - Church EIN
  - Tax-deductible statement

**Annual Statements:**
- We will send annual giving statements by January 31 of each year
- Statements include all donations made in the previous calendar year
- Statements are sent to the email address on file

**Lost Receipts:**
- You can view all donation history in the App under "Donation History"
- Contact donations@royalsignet.church to request copies of receipts

### 7.4 Non-Deductible Contributions
The following are NOT tax-deductible:
- Payments for goods or services (e.g., event tickets sold separately)
- Donations to individuals (not applicable through this App)
- Contributions to political campaigns

**All donations made through this App are tax-deductible contributions to Royal Signet Church.**

### 7.5 Substantiation Requirements
For tax purposes, the IRS requires:
- **Under $250:** Bank statement or receipt
- **$250 or more:** Written acknowledgment from church (we provide automatically)
- **$500 or more:** Same as above, plus Form 8283 if non-cash property
- **$5,000 or more of non-cash:** Qualified appraisal required

Since all donations through the App are cash contributions, you only need the receipt we provide.
