**Effective Date:** November 17, 2025

## Introduction

Royal Signet Church ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Royal Signet Church mobile application (the "App").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not use the App.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information You Provide

When you use the App, we may collect the following personal information that you voluntarily provide:

**Account Information:**
- Full name
- Email address
- Password (encrypted)
- Phone number (optional)
- Profile photo (optional)
- Date of birth (optional)
- Address (optional)

**Donation Information:**
- Donation amount
- Donation type (tithe, offering, special gift)
- Payment method information (processed securely by Stripe)
- Donation notes or messages
- Donation history

**Event Registration Information:**
- Event RSVP responses
- Special requirements or notes

**Prayer Requests:**
- Prayer request text
- Prayer responses

**Profile Information:**
- Church membership status
- Life group preferences
- Communication preferences

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use the App, we automatically collect certain information:

**Device Information:**
- Device type and model
- Operating system and version
- Unique device identifiers
- Mobile network information
- IP address

**Usage Information:**
- App features used
- Time spent on different screens
- Navigation patterns
- Search queries
- Content viewed

**Location Information (Optional):**
- Approximate location (based on IP address)
- Precise location (only if you grant permission for event check-ins)

### 1.3 Information from Third Parties

**Google Sign-In:**
- If you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile photo from Google

**Payment Information:**
- Payment card information is collected and processed by Stripe (our payment processor) and is not stored on our servers

## 3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

### 3.1 With Service Providers

We share information with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate the App:

**Firebase (Google Cloud Platform):**
- Purpose: Authentication, database, file storage, cloud functions
- Data Shared: All account and usage data
- Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

**Stripe:**
- Purpose: Payment processing
- Data Shared: Payment information, donation amounts, email
- Privacy Policy: https://stripe.com/privacy

**SendGrid (Twilio):**
- Purpose: Email delivery
- Data Shared: Email address, name, donation information
- Privacy Policy: https://www.twilio.com/legal/privacy

**Google Analytics:**
- Purpose: App usage analytics
- Data Shared: Device information, usage patterns (anonymized)
- Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### 3.2 For Legal Reasons

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to:
- Court orders or legal processes
- Government requests
- Protection of rights, property, or safety
- Fraud prevention

### 3.3 With Your Consent

We may share your information with third parties when you give us explicit consent to do so.

### 3.4 Within Church Leadership (Limited)

Church staff and authorized volunteers may access:
- Donation records (for acknowledgment and tax purposes)
- Event registrations (for planning purposes)
- Prayer requests (to pray for and respond to requests)

Access is limited to those with a legitimate need and subject to confidentiality obligations.

## 4. Data Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously and implement appropriate technical and organizational measures:

### 4.1 Security Measures
- **Encryption:** All data transmitted between the App and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
- **Authentication:** Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms
- **Access Controls:** Limited access to personal data based on role and necessity
- **Firebase Security Rules:** Database access is restricted based on user authentication
- **PCI Compliance:** Payment processing through Stripe meets PCI-DSS standards
- **Regular Security Audits:** We regularly review and update our security practices

### 4.2 Payment Security
- We do NOT store credit card numbers on our servers
- All payment information is processed directly by Stripe
- Stripe is PCI-DSS Level 1 certified (highest security standard)

### 4.3 Your Responsibility
- Keep your password confidential
- Use a strong, unique password
- Log out after using shared devices
- Report any suspicious activity immediately

**Important:** While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

---

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations:

- **Account Information:** Retained while your account is active and for 3 years after account closure
- **Donation Records:** Retained for 7 years for tax compliance purposes
- **Prayer Requests:** Retained for 1 year or until you request deletion
- **Usage Data:** Retained for 2 years for analytics purposes

You may request deletion of your account and data at any time (see Section 8).

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights:

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have additional rights under GDPR:
- Right to data portability
- Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority
- Right to withdraw consent at any time

## 7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
